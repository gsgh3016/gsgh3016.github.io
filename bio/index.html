Hi! Welcome to Chan's blog.

I was born and raised in Jeju, South Korea in 1998.
I'm always trying to being ambitious explorer.

I am a student at Pusan National University, with a major in Electronic Engineering and a minor in Computer Science.
In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also a passionate musician and former guitarist for the band 4.5F.
Throughout my music career, I have composed numerous songs and released two albums.

As I continue to explore the intersection of technology and music, I have become particularly interested in the fields of Digital Signal Processing (DSP) in audio and Deep Learning.
My ultimate goal is to create a new genre of music by Human-AI interface.

Through this blog, I hope to share my journey of combining my love for music and technology.
From exploring the latest developments in DSP and Deep Learning to sharing my own experiments and projects, I aim to provide a unique perspective on how these fields can come together to create something truly innovative and exciting.

I welcome you to join me on this exciting journey, as we explore the possibilities of Human-AI interface in music and beyond.

Let's rock the world with our creativity and technology!
